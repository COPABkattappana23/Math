Factorial

The factorial of a number is the multiplication of all the numbers between 1 and the number itself. It is written like this: n!. So the factorial of 2 is 2! (= 1 × 2). To calculate a factorial you can click "Find Factorial"

Prime Number

A whole number greater than 1 that cannot be exactly divided by any whole number other than itself and 1 (e.g. 2, 3, 5, 7, 11). To find Click "Find Prime number"

Celcius

Celsius is a scale (sometimes called Centigrade) that is used in many countries as a unit of measurement for temperature. In order to convert Fahrenheit to Celsius, we use the formula, °C = (°F - 32) × 5/9. To find click "Find Celcius"

Percentage

A number or a ratio that can be expressed as a fraction of 100 is defined as a percentage in the field of mathematics. In order to find the percentage of a particular number, we simply need to divide it by the whole and then multiply it by 100. The official symbol of percentage is %.To find click "Percentage Calculator"
